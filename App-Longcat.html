LONGCAT

Longcat is a puzzle game for SymbOS.

The original version has been developed by Martin Magni as a browser and smartphone app:
https://play.fancade.com/longcat

Later it has been ported to the Amstrad CPC by Retro Parla under the name "Loganto" in march 2025:
https://www.retroparla.com/index.php/2025/02/longato-un-sencillo-juego-para-cpc/

Finally it was ported to SymbOS by Prodatron/SymbiosiS, as a personal challenge to program a complete game for SymbOS in less than a day. It uses different graphics and the levels converted and created by Retro Parla.

1200-Playing


Your goal is to move the cat over the free fields and fill the whole map with your growing and growing tale.

When you ended in a dead end the level will restart. If you are successful, you will continue with the next level.

A total amount of 99 levels is available. You can select a level directly with the arrow and "Go" buttons.

Use the cursor keys top control the cat.

You will hear PSG or wavetable sound, if the Sound Daemon is running.
